Last week, you spent time imagining what you want your life to look and feel like.

This week, we're becoming curious about the journey itself. Chances are, you've tried to make changes before. Some worked for a while. Some didn't. Some never got off the ground at all.

Rather than judging those experiences, we're going to learn from them.

This week's journal prompt

“When have I successfully created positive change in my life before, and what helped me succeed?”

You may also notice patterns that got in the way. Be curious about them rather than critical. Every obstacle contains useful information.

The goal this week isn't to figure everything out. It's to begin understanding how you work when you're moving towards something that matters.
