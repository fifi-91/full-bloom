Hi [first name],

Over the past few weeks, you've explored where you are, where you want to go and why now matters.

This week we turn our attention to identity. Because often, the biggest obstacle isn't a lack of ability. It's an old story about who we believe ourselves to be.

Perhaps you've carried labels for years. The people-pleaser. The perfectionist. The one who always puts everyone else first. The one who isn't ready yet.

This week's journal prompt

“If I fully trusted myself, who would I be becoming?”

There is no need to force an answer. Just notice what comes up.

The person you're becoming may already be quietly present. She may simply need more room to speak.
