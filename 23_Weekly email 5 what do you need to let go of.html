What do you need to let go of?

Not everything can come with you.

Limiting beliefs, habits and patterns

This week is about noticing what no longer supports the woman you are becoming.

Hi [first name],

By now, you have begun to see the shape of the life you want and the identity you are growing into.

This week, we gently turn towards the things that may be weighing you down: old beliefs, habits, relationships, patterns or ways of coping that once protected you but no longer serve where you are going.

Letting go does not have to be dramatic. Sometimes it begins with simply telling the truth about what is costing you peace, energy or self-trust.

This week's journal prompt

“What am I carrying that no longer belongs in the life I am creating?”

Be gentle with yourself as you reflect. Some patterns stayed for a reason. We are not here to shame them, only to decide whether they still deserve a place.
