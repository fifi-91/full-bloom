Before we begin

How your Digital Tracker works.

A simple guide to getting the most out of your tracker.

01

Move slowly

There is no pressure to rush through the prompts or finish everything perfectly. Allow yourself time to think and return if needed.

02

Reflect consistently

Small moments of attention each week will take you further than occasional bursts of motivation or overthinking.

03

Stay open

Some reflections may feel clear immediately, while others may feel uncomfortable or unfamiliar at first. Let yourself stay curious.

04

Arrive honestly

You do not need to sound wise, certain, or positive. The truest answer is always the right one — even if it is messy.
