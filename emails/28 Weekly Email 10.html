Resilience and self-compassion

This week, we practise staying with ourselves.

Staying grounded through setbacks and wobbles

Resilience is not about being unshakeable. It is about knowing how to return to yourself.

Hi [first name],

As you experiment, take action and move towards the life you want, there will naturally be moments that feel uncertain or uncomfortable.

This week is about building the emotional tools that help you stay grounded when things do not go exactly to plan.

Self-compassion is not softness in the weak sense. It is a powerful way of refusing to abandon yourself when you are learning, stretching or recovering from a wobble.

This week's journal prompt

“How can I speak to myself with more kindness when things feel difficult?”

Notice the voice you use with yourself when something feels hard. Would you speak that way to someone you love?

This week, let resilience mean coming back gently instead of criticising yourself for drifting.
