By now, you've explored your vision, your motivations, your identity and the boundaries that protect what matters most.

This week is about creating an environment that supports the life you're building.

Lasting change rarely comes from willpower alone. It is often supported by simple routines, thoughtful habits and daily choices that gently guide us back towards ourselves.

This week's journal prompt

“What daily habits, routines or environments would make it easier for me to become the person I want to be?”

Think about the spaces you spend time in, the people you surround yourself with, the rituals that ground you and the habits that either support or undermine your goals.

You do not need to redesign your entire life this week. One small, supportive change is enough.
