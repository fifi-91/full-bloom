Readiness, urgency and willingness to change

Change becomes possible when we stop waiting for a perfect future version of ourselves to begin.

Hi [first name],

By now, you've started to clarify what you want and where you hope to go.

This week invites a different question: why now?

Out of all the weeks, months and years that have passed, something led you to say yes to yourself at this particular moment. That matters.

Readiness doesn't mean feeling fearless. Most meaningful change begins while uncertainty is still sitting beside us.

This week's journal prompt

“What is telling me that now is the right time to make this change?”

Notice what feels urgent, what feels important and what you're no longer willing to tolerate.

Sometimes the answer isn't dramatic. Sometimes it's simply a quiet knowing that things cannot continue exactly as they are.
