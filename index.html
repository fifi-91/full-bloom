The Full Bloom Method

A structured 12-week journey — not another self-help loop.

Reflect → Rebuild → Rise. Three phases, twelve weekly 1:1 sessions, designed so change actually sticks.

Phase 1 · Weeks 1–3

Reflect

Foundation

We get honest about where you are. Map the patterns, beliefs and silent rules running your life. You leave Phase 1 with a clear picture of what you want — and what’s been keeping you stuck.

  • Life audit across 4 pillars
  • Values & vision document
  • Personalised 12-week roadmap
Phase 2 · Weeks 4–8

Rebuild

Action

You start living the change instead of just talking about it. We build aligned habits, dismantle the blocks, and practise the new behaviours week-on-week with full accountability.

  • Weekly action goals & review
  • Confidence + boundary tools
  • Voice-note support between sessions
Phase 3 · Weeks 9–12

Rise

Integration

We lock it in. Your new habits, mindset and self-trust become the default. You leave with a 6-month forward plan and the toolkit to coach yourself when life inevitably shifts again.

  • Integration plan + relapse-proofing
  • Personal toolkit you keep for life
  • 30-day post-programme check-in
